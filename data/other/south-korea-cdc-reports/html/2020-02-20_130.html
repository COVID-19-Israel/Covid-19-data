The Updates of COVID-19 in Republic of Korea


○ As of 20 February, 2020, 31 additional cases are confirmed. one of them are identified in Seoul and the rest are in Daegu and Gyeongbuk province. Out of 30 cases from Daegu and Gyeongbuk province, 23 are linked with Shincheonji Church of Jesus, The Temple of the Tabernacle of the Testimony, the one that 31st case is associated with; two are with Cheongdo Daenam hospital and the rest five are curretly being investigated.
