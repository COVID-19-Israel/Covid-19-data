Case definition(5th update)

Confirmed case

- Laboratory-confirmed case with nCoV PCR regardless of clinical symptoms

Suspected case

- Anyone who develops fever or respiratory symptoms within 14 days after returning from China

- Anyone who develops fever or respiratory symptoms within 14 days after having a close contact with a confirmed case

- Anyone suspected of nCoV by clinician based on a travel history to affected countries and clinical symptoms

Patient Under Investigation(PUI)

- No longer used
